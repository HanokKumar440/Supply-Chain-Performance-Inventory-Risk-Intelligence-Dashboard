# Dataset Overview

## Dataset Name

DataCo Smart Supply Chain Dataset

## Dataset Purpose

This dataset contains supply chain transaction data including orders, products, customers, shipping details, delivery performance, and profitability metrics.

The dataset will be used to analyze supply chain operations and identify opportunities to improve delivery performance, operational efficiency, and business profitability.

---

## Main Business Areas

### Orders
Tracks customer orders and order-level transactions.

### Products
Contains product information including category and pricing.

### Customers
Contains customer demographic and segmentation data.

### Shipping & Logistics
Tracks shipment schedules, delivery times, shipping modes, and delivery risks.

### Geography
Contains regional and market information for location-based analysis.

### Financial Performance
Contains sales, profit, and order value metrics.

---

## Planned Analysis Areas

- Executive KPI Reporting
- Delivery Performance Analysis
- Product Performance Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Regional Performance Analysis
- Inventory & Risk Intelligence

---

## Project Goal

Build an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution using SQL and Power BI to transform operational supply chain data into actionable insights for business decision-making.
